Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

At ChamaSpace, we collect and process various types of information to deliver, enhance, and personalize our services. This section outlines the categories of information we collect, including but not limited to:

a) Personal Identifiable Information (PII):
Full Names: We collect your full legal names to personalize your experience and ensure accurate identification.
ID Number: Your identification number is collected to comply with regulatory requirements and verify your identity.
Address: We collect your postal and physical address to facilitate account-related communication and transactions.
Phone Number: Your contact number is essential for communication, security, and account verification purposes.
Email Address: We may request your email address for account-related notifications and support communication.
Mpesa Registered Number: If applicable, we collect your Mpesa registered number for payment and transaction purposes.

b) Additional Information:
Source of Referral: We may ask how you heard about ChamaSpace to understand the effectiveness of our marketing efforts and improve our services.
Cookies: We utilize cookies for website functionality, analytics, and personalized content delivery. Please refer to our Cookies and Tracking Technologies section for more details.

c) Children's Privacy:
ChamaSpace services are intended for individuals who are at least 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect or process information from individuals under the age of 18.

2. How We Use Collected Information

ChamaSpace utilizes the information we collect for various purposes, ensuring the delivery of efficient and personalized services. Here's an overview of how we use the collected information:

a) Mpesa Information for Transactions:
Processing Withdrawals and Deposits: Your Mpesa registered number is essential for processing financial transactions, including withdrawals and deposits, ensuring a seamless and secure payment experience.

b) Account Opening and Verification:
Account Creation: Full names, ID number, address, phone number, and email address are collected during the account creation process to establish and personalize your ChamaSpace account.
Verification: We use collected information, including your ID number, for identity verification purposes to comply with regulatory requirements and ensure the security of your account.

c) KYC (Know Your Customer):
Compliance: We collect certain personal information, such as your ID number, to adhere to Know Your Customer (KYC) regulations and foster a secure and compliant financial environment.
