Cookie Policy

Last Updated: June 11, 2025

1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how ChamaSpace (a product of Jeld Investment LTD) uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website or use our mobile application.

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website or use an app. They help us recognize your device, remember your preferences, and improve your overall experience.

2. Why Do We Use Cookies?

We use cookies for several important purposes:

a) Essential Functionality:

  • To enable core features like login sessions, security, and navigation.

b) Performance & Analytics:

  • To understand how users interact with our platform so we can improve speed, usability, and reliability.

c) Personalization:

  • To remember your settings and preferences (e.g., language, theme).

d) Security:

  • To detect and prevent fraud, spam, and unauthorized access.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following categories of cookies:

a) Strictly Necessary Cookies:

  • These are essential for the website to function. Without them, services like secure login or transaction processing would not work. These do not require your consent.

b) Analytics Cookies:

  • These help us measure traffic and usage trends (e.g., via Google Analytics or similar tools). They collect anonymous data about how you use ChamaSpace.

c) Functional Cookies:

  • These remember your choices (e.g., username, region) to enhance your experience.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may allow trusted third-party service providers (such as payment processors, analytics tools, or customer support platforms) to place cookies on your device. These third parties are contractually obligated to use cookies only for the purposes we specify and in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

5. How to Manage or Disable Cookies

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • Block all cookies
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Receive alerts when a cookie is placed

Note: If you disable essential cookies, you may not be able to log in, use secure features, or access certain parts of ChamaSpace.

6. Changes to This Cookie Policy

If you have questions about our use of cookies, please contact us at: info@chamaspace.com. By using ChamaSpace, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy.
