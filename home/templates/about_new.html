About ChamaSpace

The simple, safe and secure way to save and grow your wealth

Our Mission

ChamaSpace is designed to simplify the management of chamas, merry-go-rounds, and saving towards personal and group goals. We help you and your Chama perfect your journey of saving and growing your wealth together.

What We Offer

  • Personal Savings Goals: Create and track individual savings objectives
  • Group Savings: Collaborate with others on shared financial goals
  • Chama Management: Complete tools for managing group finances
  • Competitive Interest Rates: Earn up to 12% annual interest
  • Secure Platform: Your money and data are protected

Why Choose ChamaSpace?

We understand the importance of community in achieving financial goals. Our platform combines traditional chama practices with modern technology to provide a seamless saving experience.
